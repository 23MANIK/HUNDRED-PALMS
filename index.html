Welcome to Hundred Palms

Chef Patron Joan Smith and Executive Chef François Lemoine have teamed up to open one of the most exciting restaurants in Anytown.

Our speciality is delicious cuisine created with the ingenuity and passion inspired by the fantastic flavours of India itself. We take great pride in improving upon your favourite Indian dishes in new and creative ways.
